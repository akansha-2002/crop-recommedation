About Our Crop Recommendation System

Our crop recommendation system is an AI-powered tool designed to help farmers optimize their crop yield and increase their profits. Using a combination of machine learning algorithms and agricultural data, our system is able to recommend the best crops for a particular region based on factors such as soil type, weather patterns, and historical yield data.

To use this model, farmer has to go to the nearby soil testing center and bring the value of the amount of Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium and pH level of the soil with the Temperature of the prevailing weather and the amount of rainfall in mm.

Our system is easy to use and provides farmers with personalized recommendations tailored to their specific needs. By using our system, farmers can make informed decisions about which crops to plant and when to plant them, resulting in higher crop yields and increased profits.

Whether you are a small-scale farmer or a large agricultural operation, our crop recommendation system can help you optimize your crop yield and increase your profits. Try it out today and see the difference it can make!
